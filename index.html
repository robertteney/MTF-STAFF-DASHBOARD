What is the MTF?

The MTF (Maximum Time Frame) period is a designated timeframe after a student graduates to continue preparing for and taking their certification exams!

MTF Voucher Limitations

Please keep the following critical policy limitations in mind:

  • Students can only have ONE active voucher at a time.
  • Students are approved for up to a 4th attempt at an exam.
  • Any certification voucher requested must be completed by the student's designated MTF expiration date.
